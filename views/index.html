Frequently asked questions

What is the JungleBox?

It is a VR room that lets you access Jungle online marketplace. It can be intalled at your home, or anywhere else you'd like to have it. A truly VR experience, inside you can touch, smell, taste, see and hear our products and services.

How will the JungleBox facilitate my daily life?

It will make everything tangibly available at your home. No more trips to the post office, no more trips to cafes, ... In fact, you don't have to leave your home at all, but it's highly recommended that you do.

Is JungleBox safe?

We understand that technology can have negative physical and mental impact on our bodies. At JungleBox, we understand the impact that AI and attention-monopolizing experiences can have on our bodies, so we continuously monitor the effect of our technology on physical and cognitive development

What are the different membership options?

You can receive a complimentary version of JungleBox, but it will be tied to your Jungle account, so all purchases will have to be done via our systems. You can also choose to purchase JungleBox hardware, which permits you to use 3rd-party services.

Is JungleBox accessible?

We want to permit people of all abilities and economic backgrounds to have full experience of our technology. Our Virtual Assistant dynamically adjust to communicate in exactly the way you want him.

Is JungleBox a real thing?

No, it is a speculative concept submitted as a Final Project for Communication and Technology class at NYU Abu Dhabi in Fall 2016
